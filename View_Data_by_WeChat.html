4.8 View Data by WeChat

1. Introduction

You can also view data on WeChat by following Iammeter WeChat ID. In this case, you don't need to install any APP for more convenience.

2. View Energy Data by WeChat

Step1, Scan the below QRcode to follow Iammeter: https://leweidoc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com

Step2, Sign up by by following steps. Please skip this step if you already have an Iammeter account. https://leweidoc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com

Step3, Login via WeChat after sign up. https://leweidoc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com

Step4, View your data on WeChat https://leweidoc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com

Note

If you have a WeChat account, please follow our IAMMETER WeChat public ID. https://leweidoc.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com
